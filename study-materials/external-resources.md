## Key Community Insights

1. The single most tested concept: **programmatic enforcement vs prompt-based guidance**
2. Over 45% of exam focuses on D1 (agentic architecture) + D3 (Claude Code config)
3. Exam "consistently rewards deterministic solutions over probabilistic ones"
4. One candidate passed in 5 days with daily hands-on Claude experience
5. Recommended timeline: 6-10 weeks for experienced devs, 2-4 months for newcomers
6. Exam omits: cost optimization for multi-agent, model selection tradeoffs, observability practices
